Welcome to Happy Ocean!

Hi, there! I'm Otter, the Happy Ocean lifeguard! I'm here to guide you as you explore our beach.

Getting Around

Check out the map icon at the top left corner. If you're ever lost, just open the map and choose where you want to go.

Meet Our Mascots

Happy Ocean has elected five mascots to represent our beach:

  • Humpback Whale
  • Sergeant Major Fish
  • Green Sea Turtle
  • Laysan Albatross
  • California Sea Lion

Each of them has a story to tell about how plastic pollution in the ocean affects their lives.

Beach Cleanup

We've organized a beach cleanup party and you're welcome to participate if you like by picking up any garbage you find along your way. Thanks and have fun!
